Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

TL;DR: Project Launcher runs locally on your Mac. Your source code and project files never leave your machine. We only collect minimal data needed for licensing and crash reporting.

1. Who We Are

Project Launcher is developed by Vivek Yadav. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use the Project Launcher application ("the App").

2. Data We Collect

We categorize data into what stays on your device and what is transmitted.

Data that stays on your device (never transmitted)

  • Your project files, source code, and git repositories
  • Project health scores and analytics computed locally
  • App preferences and configuration
  • Dashboard layout and customizations

Data we may collect

Data Purpose Stored by
Anonymous user ID License validation Our servers
Email address Subscription & billing Paddle
Payment information Processing payments Paddle
Crash reports Bug fixing Our servers
App version & OS version Compatibility & support Our servers

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the minimal data we collect to:

  • Validate your Pro subscription status
  • Process payments and manage billing through Paddle
  • Diagnose and fix crashes and bugs
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve the App's stability and performance

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Paddle — Payment processing and subscription management. Paddle acts as our Merchant of Record and processes your payment information under their own Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your local data is stored on your device and protected by your operating system's security features. Data transmitted to our servers is encrypted in transit using TLS.

We do not store your payment details — Paddle handles all payment data in PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure.

6. Data Retention

  • Local data: Remains on your device until you delete the App or clear its data.
  • Subscription data: Retained while your subscription is active and for a reasonable period after cancellation for billing and legal purposes.
  • Crash reports: Automatically deleted after 90 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct any inaccurate data.
  • Delete your data — contact us and we will remove your information from our systems.
  • Export your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent at any time by uninstalling the App.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@projectlauncher.dev.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the App or at this URL. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at support@projectlauncher.dev.
