Last updated: April 4, 2026

Our promise: If you're not satisfied with Project Launcher Pro, we'll give you a full refund within 14 days of purchase. No questions asked.

14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We want you to be completely happy with Project Launcher Pro. If the App doesn't meet your expectations, you can request a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase.

What's Covered

  • First-time purchases — Your initial Pro subscription payment is fully refundable within 14 days.
  • Technical issues — If the App doesn't work on your system and we can't resolve the issue, we'll refund your payment regardless of when it was made.
  • Accidental purchases — Unintended charges are refunded promptly.

What's Not Covered

  • Renewal payments — Monthly renewals after the first 14 days are not eligible for refund, but you can cancel anytime to prevent future charges.
  • Abuse — Repeated refund requests (e.g., subscribing and refunding multiple times) may be declined.
  • Promo/trial periods — Free trial periods don't involve a charge, so there's nothing to refund.

How to Request a Refund

  1. Email us at support@projectlauncher.dev with the subject line "Refund Request".
  2. Include your Paddle receipt or the email address used for purchase.
  3. We'll confirm your refund within 2 business days.
  4. The refund will appear on your statement within 5-10 business days, depending on your payment provider.

After a Refund

Once your refund is processed:

  • Your Pro subscription will be cancelled immediately.
  • You'll revert to the Free plan.
  • Your projects and local data remain intact — nothing is deleted.
  • You can re-subscribe at any time.

Cancellation vs. Refund

A cancellation stops future billing — you keep Pro access until the end of your current period. A refund returns your most recent payment and ends Pro access immediately.

To cancel without a refund, you can do so from within the App or by contacting support.

Contact

Questions about refunds? Reach us at support@projectlauncher.dev. We respond within 1 business day.
